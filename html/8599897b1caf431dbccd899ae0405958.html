Experiment No: 7

Batch reactor

Aim:

Conduct a simulation experiment and determine the rate constant for the saponification of ethyl acetate (A) with NaOH (B) in a batch reactor at a specified temperature. To study the effect of temperature on the rate constant. To determine the frequency factor and activation energy.

CH3COOC2H5 (A)+ NaOH (B)-> CH3COONa + C2H5OH It is an elementary reaction
Ethyl acetate is the limiting reactant

Apparatus:Measuring cylinders, stop watch

Procedure for conducting the experiment:
  1. Standardise the NaOH stock solution and note down its concentration. Note down the strength of ethyl acetate stock solution.

  3. Add the calculated volume of NaOH into the reactor. Start the stirrer. Switch on the conductivity meter.
  4. Switch on the heater and set the temperature at the desired value. Pump the water from heating tank for circulation in the jacket .So the temperature in the reactor is maintained at the required temperature.
